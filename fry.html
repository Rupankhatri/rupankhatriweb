Chicken Fry Pack Recipe

This is a recipe for a delicious and crispy chicken fry pack. It is made with chicken breast, flour, breadcrumbs, eggs, and spices.

  • 1 pound chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a shallow dish, combine flour, breadcrumbs, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. In a separate shallow dish, whisk together egg.
  3. Dip the chicken pieces in the egg, then coat them in the breadcrumb mixture.
  4. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Fry the chicken pieces in batches for 3-4 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Serve immediately with your favorite dipping sauce.
