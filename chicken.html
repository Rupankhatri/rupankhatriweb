Chicken Burger Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheese
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 tomato slices

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground chicken, onion, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Form mixture into 4 patties.
  4. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Cook patties for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  6. Place patties on a baking sheet and top each with a slice of cheese.
  7. Bake for 5 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
  8. Assemble burgers with buns, lettuce, tomato, and desired condiments.
