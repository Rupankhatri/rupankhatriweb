Special Beef Burger Recipe

This is a recipe for a delicious and special beef burger. It is made with fresh ground beef, cheese, bacon, lettuce, tomato, and onion.

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup mustard
  • 4 slices cheese
  • 4 slices bacon, cooked
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Form the mixture into 4 patties.
  4. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Cook the patties for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  5. In a small bowl, combine ketchup, mayonnaise, and mustard.
  6. To assemble the burgers, spread the bottom buns with the ketchup mixture. Top with lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, bacon, and patty.
  7. Place the burgers on a baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted.
  8. Serve immediately.
